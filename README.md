What I build

Lead Generation
Capturing leads is one thing. Actually doing something with them is another. I set up workflows that pull leads from your site into Google Sheets (or wherever), fire off an email or SMS right away, ping your sales team in Slack, and score them in the background — all without anyone having to click anything.

CRM Automation — Hubspot / Salesforce
Form submissions syncing to your CRM, leads getting routed to the right person, deal stages updating automatically, contacts getting enriched. The boring stuff that no one wants to do manually but somehow always falls through the cracks.

E-commerce — Shopify / WooCommerce
Order processing, inventory syncing across platforms, auto-generated invoices, and follow-up emails that go out at the right time. Useful if you're running a store and tired of doing the same five things every morning.

Support Bots — Slack / Telegram / Discord
Ticket systems, FAQ bots, customer notifications, escalation to a real human when needed. Built to handle the volume without getting in the way.

Scheduling & Calendar
Calendly syncs, reminder sequences, team calendar updates, follow-ups — that whole category of stuff that takes way too long to manage by hand.

AI Workflows — OpenAI / Claude / others
Content generation pipelines, data analysis, AI support agents, document summarization. If you want to plug an LLM into your existing tools without building everything from scratch, this is what I do.
